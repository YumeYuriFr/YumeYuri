# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2025

## Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This website is designed to operate without collecting, storing, or processing personal data.

By using this website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- IP addresses
- Device identifiers
- Location data
- Cookies
- Analytics data
- Usage statistics

## Server-Side Storage

We do **not** store any user data on our servers.

No databases, logs, or user profiles are maintained for visitors of this website.

## Client-Side Storage

We do **not** store any information on your device.

This website does not use:

- Cookies
- Local Storage
- Session Storage
- IndexedDB
- Browser caches for user tracking purposes

## Third-Party Services

This website does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or data collection services.

## Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not sell, share, transfer, or disclose any information to third parties.

## Children's Privacy

This website does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 or any other age threshold defined by applicable laws.

## Security

As no user information is collected or stored, there is no personal data at risk of unauthorized access through this website.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
